Demo (Unity 2D), 2023

Procedural Illumination

In this project, I created a demo featuring a character that walks procedurally to the rhythm of "Nadie Sabe" by Bad Bunny. The character's movement is synced with the song, moving forward only when the singer sings. This added a dynamic and engaging aspect to the demo, ensuring that the character's actions were perfectly in time with the music.

I utilized a frequency detector to enhance the visual experience. This detector changed the lighting, turning lights on and off and altering their colors based on the song's frequencies. Additionally, the demo included symbolic actions tied to the song's lyrics, with the character appearing in different poses and performing various actions as the song progressed. This project showcases my ability to integrate audio with character animation and lighting effects, creating an immersive and interactive experience.

Personal Demo (Unity AR/VR), 2024

BasketbAR

BasketbAR is an augmented reality project developed in Unity 3D using Vuforia. The game involves shooting basketballs into a virtual hoop that appears on top of a Coca-Cola can, transforming the can's top into the hoop. I integrated Vuforia with Unity and the can, ensuring a smooth AR experience. I also developed the physics for throwing the basketball, including a curve effect on the ball’s trajectory.

Additionally, I created a comprehensive scoring system to track player performance and enhance the game's competitive aspect. This project highlights my skills in AR integration, physics simulation, and game mechanics development, showcasing my technical expertise in Unity 3D and Vuforia, as well as my creativity in designing engaging gameplay elements.
